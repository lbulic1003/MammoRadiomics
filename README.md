### MammoRadiomics: Predictive AI Software for Personalized Breast Cancer Treatment

MammoRadiomics is an AI-based software designed for breast cancer neoadjuvant chemotherapy (NAC) outcome prediction based on radiomic analysis of magnetic resonance images. The insights gained from highly accurate NAC response prediction, as produced by the MammoRadiomics software, offer the potential for further breast cancer management personalization.
